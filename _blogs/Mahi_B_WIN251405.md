Since beginning my internship, it has been an incredibly productive experience. The program is structured into three clear stages: starting with the ViBe Dashboard for mastering the MERN stack, moving into deep-dive case studies, and concluding with a hands-on project. I have wrapped up the video modules and am currently immersed in the case study phase. It’s been a balanced journey of ups and downs, but the format is consistently engaging—challenging enough to keep me occupied and deeply motivated to learn.

The pace here is real; the deadlines and submission requirements definitely keep us on our toes, ensuring we stay disciplined and focused. However, it’s not just about solo coding. The frequent stand-up sessions have been a highlight, providing a great space to interact with my peers, share our unique experiences, and grow together. To top it off, the quizzes integrated into the flow are a fun, engaging way to test our knowledge and keep the learning momentum high.

Diving deep into the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React, and Node.js) has been a game-changer. Moving from theoretical concepts to seeing how these technologies integrate in real-world scenarios has significantly leveled up my development skills. It is exactly the kind of modern industry experience I was looking for, and I am excited to carry this knowledge into my upcoming hands-on project.

A Note of Gratitude

I want to extend a sincere thank you to Prof. Sudarshan Iyengar, Vicharanashala (Lab for Education Design), ANNAM.AI, and other mentors for putting together such a well-structured program. I’m looking forward to tackling the rest of my case studies and making the most of this opportunity!
